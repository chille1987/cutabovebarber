About Us

Cut Above Academy (CABA) is an extension of the Cut Above the Rest brand, which has been established for 28 years with a highly successful barber shop on Barking Road, East Ham. Award-winning course director Patrick Phipps has an impressive track record which spans over two decades of teaching other barbers, many of whom are now successful business owners and stylists.

Patrick

Patrick regularly works in his barber shop to ensure that he stays one step ahead of styles being fashioned in the industry and on the streets. He also understands the importance of client care and has put his skills to use for adverts, music videos and many celebrities over the years, including singers, fashion photographers, DJs, movie stars, footballers and more!
