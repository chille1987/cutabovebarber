Cut Above Barber Academy

Intro

Cut Above Barber Academy (CABA) is an extension of the Cut Above the Rest brand, which has been established for 28 years with a highly successful barber shop on Barking Road, East Ham.

Award-winning course director Patrick Phipps has an impressive track record which spans over two decades of teaching other barbers, many of whom are now successful business owners and stylists.

Why CABA?

See it, think it, feel it and you will become it. CABA is here to help you achieve your dreams. Now you can’t bet that!

Our Courses

Caba’s courses teach basic, modern and traditional barber skills. You’ll learn about cut-throat wet shaving, advanced scissor skills and clipper techniques to suit all hair types, textures and styles.

Beginners course

The Fundamentals of Barbering course is aimed at all ages and a CABA certificate will be provided on completion.

Course Length:

Four weeks, three hours a day, two days per week.

Models will be provided.

Intermediate course

This is an intermediate course for students who wish to progress from Level 1 onwards.

Course Length:

Advanced course
