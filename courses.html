CABA Courses

Caba’s courses teach basic, modern and traditional barber skills. You’ll learn about cut-throat wet shaving, advanced scissor skills and clipper techniques to suit all hair types, textures and styles.

We endeavour to provide a course for everybody, from beginners and people returning to work to refreshers: this is our aim and we aim very high.

We keep our classes small to ensure that you get the excellent standard of tuition you deserve. If you show ambition and determination on our Level 1, 2 and 3 courses, you will be extended even further to do an Advanced Barbering Skills course that covers cut throat wet shaving, advanced scissor skills and clipper techniques and pattern work to suit all hair types, textures and styles. Beginners course

This Level 1 course is aimed at all ages and a Caba certificate will be provided on completion.

The course includes

  • The Basics

    The tools of the trade, how to use them, tool care and sterilisation.

  • Theory

    Introduction to hair structure and grades; how to hold your tools – clipper angles.

  • Cutting

    Mastering the two fundamental shapes of men’s haircuts (square and contoured), shaping-up and styling, finishing, plus the basic one-length cut on different hair grades.

  • Customer care

    How to build a client base, consultation and developing rapport, pre-cut rituals, for example, training the direction of the hair and checking scalp skin. Reassessing hairline as part of after-cut finishing.

  • Professionalism

    Maintenance of the workspace.

Intermediate course

Advanced course

This level is appropriate for highly able or older students who wish to progress to the status of Master Barber.

The course includes

  • Short back and sides (graduation)
  • The bald fade
  • High tops
  • Beard work with different hair grades
  • Introduction to pattern work

CABA Connections

Caba is creating a network of barber shops and barbers to provide work placements for learners. The academy can potentially offer employment opportunities in-house at Cut Above the Rest barber studio because Patrick is always looking for the next big talent. This is where you will get the opportunity to further strengthen and develop your skills in your new trade. As a trainee, you will not get no better real life experience of learning the trade than this.

Your learning will be entirely hands-on, simple but highly structured, and will combine traditional barbering with modern male hairdressing. As a result, you will become extremely skilled and knowledgeable about cutting and styling men’s hair.

More than a cutting hair

There is more to being a barber than just cutting hair.

You will learn more than cutting and styling hair when you enrol on CABA courses. With undivided attention from your tutor throughout, you will leave feeling confident and with strong skills at the start of your career.

We operate a well-established barber shop just two minutes’ walk from our academy. Some learners may get work experience there and even a permanent job.

Each course includes step-by-step tutorial videos to allow students to continue their learning at home and to revise and prepare for assessments.

Caba’s approach is 80% practical and our experienced tutor will give you the confidence to be able to complete a one length haircut and shape-up to a high standard. These are the most important aspects of barbering and will give you the fundamentals before moving onto the classic side parting through to the more challenging zero fade.
